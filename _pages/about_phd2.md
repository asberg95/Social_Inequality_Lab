**Halima Bouras** Halima Bouras is PhD student at Leiden University, within Social, Economic, and Organisational Psychology and worked as a lecturer for 23 years, at a university of applied sciences. She holds a master in psychology and two bachelors (B. of Education in general economics and marketing management).  
She is recipient of a doctoral grant for teachers of the Dutch Research council (NWO).

**Research interests:**
Education inequality of opportunity and social inequality in general.
