**Zafira Shabrina** <br>
A PhD student in the Department of Social, 
Economic, and Organisational Psychology at Leiden University and a lecturer at Universitas Gadjah Mada (UGM), Indonesia. 

Her PhD research is aimed at advancing the understanding of psychological and demographic factors that contribute to why self-employed workers often do not save enough, subsequently developing a behaviorally informed intervention that effectively 'nudges' them to save.
